ARTICLES

ARTICLES:

In English there are three articles a, an, and the.

'A'
'A' is use with singular countable nouns (a person, animal or thing).
which are not specifies we don't use 'a' before uncountable or plural nouns.
If a noun start with a constant sound (b,c,d,f,g,h,j,k,l,m,n,p,q,r,s,t,v,w,x,y,z).

Example:
A cat
A book
A bird
A pen
A table

'An'
'An' is used with singular countable noun which are not specific
We don't use an before uncountable or plural nouns.
If the noun startt with 'a' vowels sound (a,e,i,o,u), "an" come before the noun.

Example:
An apple
An egg
An orange

'The'
We use 'the' with all nouns(it does not matter whether the nouns the nouns are singular,plural,countable or uncountable)
